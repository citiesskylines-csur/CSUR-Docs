BETA

Roads made realistic.

CSUR is a road building system developed for the game Cities: Skylines.
It helps players to more efficiently build realistic road infrastructures and massive road networks.
Simple, Fast, and Free.

Get Start Download Beta

CSUR has over 500 assects, including four types of road: The Base roads, Shift roads, Trans roads, Ramp roads.
If none of the above roads meet your needs, you can even use the tools we provide to build your own road.
